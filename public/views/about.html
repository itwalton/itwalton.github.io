About

Ian Walton is a software developer currently living and working in Dallas, TX. After graduating from the University of North Texas, Ian started working at Sogeti as a consultant for Pier1 Imports. After a little over a year, he left to venture out on his own. He is currently doing client-work.

Ian's main areas of expertise includes writing full-stack applications with DemandWare, Ruby on Rails, NodeJS, and front-end frameworks such as AngularJS and BackboneJS. He is passionate about following the principles of Domain Driven Design and Test Driven Development.

Outside of software, Ian's passions involve coffee, fitness, eating spicy foods, and Texas Rangers games with his beautiful wife.
